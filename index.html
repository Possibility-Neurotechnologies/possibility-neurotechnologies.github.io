The future is inclusive.

Unlock new possibilities with the Think2Switch™

The Think2Switch™ is a one-of-a-kind device that translates brain signals into usable commands, letting you control real-world objects using only your thoughts!

Empowerment at the speed of thought™

With the help of a brain-sensing headset and our Think2Switch™ software, you can use the Think2Switch™ to control up to four separate switch-adapted or electrical devices. The Think2Switch™ was designed to be easy to use, so even if you have never heard of brain-controlled technologies before, the Think2Switch™ is still for you!
