How it works

Three steps. Then you're in control.

No surgery. No implants. No clinic visit. Just put on the headset, calibrate once, and start controlling the world around you.

1

Put on the headset

The Muse Athena EEG headset sits comfortably on your head. Buy it at choosemuse.com/possneuro for an exclusive 15% off.

2

Calibrate to your brain

The app learns your unique brain signal in a short guided session. Think of a mental action and Think2Switch™ learns to recognise it.

3

Start controlling

Use your mental command to play games, control smart home devices, navigate apps, and more. Most users succeed in their very first session.
