The science, simply explained

What is a brain-computer interface?

A BCI reads the electrical signals your brain produces naturally and translates them into commands — without any physical movement. Think2Switch™ uses non-invasive EEG: sensors rest on your scalp, detect your brain's activity, and translate a chosen mental state into a switch action.

Invasive BCIs

Require surgery to implant electrodes into brain tissue. High signal fidelity, significant medical risk. Not what Think2Switch™ uses.

Non-invasive EEG ← Think2Switch™

Sensors rest on the scalp. Safe, painless, and used in consumer and clinical settings for over a decade. No surgery required.
