Our mission

To bring brain-controlled technology out of research labs — and into the hands of people who need it most.

Brain-computer interface technology has existed in research labs for decades. We founded Possibility Neurotechnologies because too many people who could benefit from it have never had access to it. We're changing that — one connection at a time.

Accessibility first

We design for the people most often left out by mainstream technology. If it isn't accessible, we start over.

Grounded in science

Think2Switch™ is built on peer-reviewed neuroscience. Every design decision is evidence-based.

Human-centred design

Users, families, and clinicians shape what we build. Every feature is co-designed with our community.
